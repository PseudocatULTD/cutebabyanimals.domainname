About This Site

This website contains everything you could possibly want to know about baby animals. But you probably guessed that already.

The buttons at the top of this page will take you to pages about the animal you clicked on. Each animal page has facts, statistics and - best of all - pictures!

This site was made by two computer science students when we were bored. So a stupid idea turned into a silly website full of cute baby animals.

Anyway, let your baby animal browsing commence!
